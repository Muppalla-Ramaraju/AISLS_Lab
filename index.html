Livestock Sustainability Mantra

Sustainability for livestock systems could be conceptualized as a multi-criteria optimization problem. Food animal production systems should simultaneously improve productivity, promote health, and prevent disease in animals within the economic framework of the industry, while recognizing animal welfare, food safety, public health, and environmental sustainability. In mathematical terms its a multitrait optimization problem.

Product Format

The products from K lab could be broadly classified into three.

1

Field or empirical data to which different statistical or machine learning models are fitted for predictions.

2

Herd level complex system decision models which undergo statistic analysis for investigating economic, environmental, and epidemiological performance of herd under management strategies.

3

Customizable, dynamic visualizable back decision tools for livestock systems stakeholders
